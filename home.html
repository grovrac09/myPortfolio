Why do I want to be a software engineer?

Ever since I was little, I thought computers were cool. I'd watch Abby tap away at her keyboard on NCIS and think about the endless possibilities I could have from learning more.

I didn't get the chance to attend university, and online learning was difficult alongside work. Luckily, my employer opened an apprenticeship for software engineering, which means I have the chance to learn and work, all at once!

My targets

My end goal is to become a security engineer, and help companies defend themselves against the ever-growing threat of cybercriminals.

It's a long way in the future, but I plan to achieve this by..

  • Completing my CodeNation learning to gain my level 4 qualifiaction in software development.

  • Continuing to learn with my employer, and branch out to more services than just web.

  • Creating more projects to build my knowledge.

If you want to have some fun, you can change this sentence to anything your heart desires!
